Battle Brawlers Card Game - [Demo]

In Battle Brawlers Card Game, it's all about mastering both cards and Brawlers. The game is played with 3 bots in a turn-based format, where each player chooses from 3 types of cards (Bronze, Silver and Gold) and selects 3 unique Brawlers, each with their own types.

  • Card Phases: At the start of the game, each player places one card face-down on the board. In each subsequent turn, you can choose to either place another card or position a Brawler on an existing card.
  • Brawler Battles:When two Brawlers land on the same card, a battle begins. Each Brawler gets a boost or penalty based on the card theyâ€™re fighting on, then a mini-game triggers (Symbol-matching, Click, Turn)

The player who wins the mini-game scores 1 point. The game continues until one player collects 3 points and claims victory!

Key Features:

  • Strategic Deck Building: Choose 1 card from each tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold) and 3 Brawlers to create your ideal lineup.
  • Turn-Based Action: Outthink your opponents by playing cards and Brawlers at the perfect moment.
  • Funny Mini-Games: Every battle ends in a fast-paced mini-game, master them to secure points!

The Battle Brawlers Card Game [Demo] offers a taste of what's to come. A multiplayer support and an adventure can be considered.
