Eyefox Puzzle - A puzzle of Patterns

In Eyefox Puzzle, your goal is simple yet deceptively challenging: replicate the grid on the left by manipulating the grid on the right. Each level begins with two grids, one on the left and one on the right. The left grid shows the target configuration, while the right grid starts in a different state, with some tiles hidden and others revealed.

When you click on any tile in the right grid, that tile, along with its immediate neighbors, will flip between hidden and visible. Your task is to transform the right grid to match the left, using a limited number of moves. Each level presents a new grid layout and a different number of moves to complete the puzzle, making the challenge progressively harder.

Key Features:

  • Dynamic Puzzle Mechanics: Every move affects multiple tiles, so plan carefully!
  • Challenging Levels: Start easy, but as you advance, grids become more complex, and the number of moves more limited.
  • Strategy & Precision: It’s not just about flipping tiles—it's about doing it in the most efficient way possible.
  • Progressive Difficulty: Levels grow more intricate as you master each mechanic.
