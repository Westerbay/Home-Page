Farworld [2022]

This project is a card game build with Python in french. It was a group effort for the final semester of the first year in a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics.

The goal of the game is to choose a deck, face off against a computer opponent, and win. Your deck includes fighters, tools to boost their stats, and spells. Actions cost energy, so you'll need to strategize carefully to outplay your opponent and claim victory.
