Battle Brawlers Card Game [2022]

Battle Brawlers Card Game is a turn-based strategy game where every card, every Brawler, and every move counts!

It was inspired by a childhood game on DS called Bakugan Battle Brawlers. It is a hybrid strategy and action game. Players engage in turn-based battles by strategically placing Brawlers onto the battlefield, aiming to land them on Gate Cards. Once two Brawlers land on the same card, a battle ensues, resolved through mini-games like rapid button presses. The game emphasizes planning, timing, and skillful execution, blending tactical decision-making with fast-paced action.
