Taquin's Sudoku [2023]

This project is a game where you can play the classic 15-puzzle (sliding puzzle), Sudoku, and a new hybrid mode that combines both.

In the hybrid mode, the tiles of the sliding puzzle are hidden, and to reveal them, you must solve a Sudoku puzzle. This unique combination challenges both logic and strategy for a fresh gaming experience.
